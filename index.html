About Me

My name is Ping Jin. I am a second year master student in University of Alberta, supervised by Prof. Russell Greiner. My current research focuses on applying sparse methods to patient-specific survival curve prediction.

Research Interests

My general research interests lie in machine learning and its applications, which include bandit online learning, latent dirichlet allocation, recommendation system, and especially sparse models. I am also very interested in deep learning and its applications in computer vision.
